About Me

I'm a Robotics & Autonomous Systems Engineer passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical algorithms and real-world hardware. My work spans the full autonomy stack — from low-level sensor drivers to high-level path planning and mission control.

I specialize in robot navigation without GPS, using OpenStreetMap for environmental context combined with live RPLidar A1M8 sensor data to build real-time obstacle maps. I develop and tune A* and TangentBug planners, configuration-space visualizers, and reactive avoidance systems.
